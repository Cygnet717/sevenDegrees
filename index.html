Six Degrees

How do I play this game?

You will be given two actors and the challenge is to connect them through the movies them and others have been in.

Example Challenge:

Connect Brad Pitt to Johnny Depp

Solution:

Brad Pitt → Mr. & Mrs. Smith → Angelina Jolie → The tourist → Johnny Depp

Then you can use the Connection counter to count the steps between them:
2 steps from Brad Pitt to Johnny Depp
